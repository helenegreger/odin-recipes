Vegetarian Chili

Description

They'll never know it's vegetarian!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package frozen burger-style crumbles
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans dark red kidney beans
  • 1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. Find a large pot.
  2. Combine all the ingredients in the pot.
  3. Bring to a simmer and cover.
  4. Let the chili simmer for at least 1 hour before serving.
